Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your account data collected by RIMStudio, follow the steps below.

  1. Disconnect RIMStudio from your Instagram or Facebook account (in the app or via Meta settings).
  2. Email us at legal@rimstudio.co with the subject “Delete my data — RIMStudio”, and include your Instagram username or Page ID.
  3. We will confirm and delete data associated with your account within 30 days (usually much sooner), except data we must retain to comply with law or resolve disputes.

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
