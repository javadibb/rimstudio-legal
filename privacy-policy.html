Privacy Policy

Effective date: 29 October 2025 • Last updated: 29 October 2025

RIMStudio (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates automation and content tools, including integrations with Instagram and Facebook. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account & Contact: name, email, business profile details you provide.
  • Instagram/Facebook Data (only with your permission): page/account IDs, basic profile fields, content metadata (e.g., captions, comment text, media IDs), and events required to deliver features such as comment monitoring, auto-replies, or analytics.
  • Usage & Technical: device/browser type, IP address, timestamps, logs, and error diagnostics.
  • Customer Support: messages and attachments you send us.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the services (e.g., publish content, respond to comments, analytics).
  • Authenticate requests to Instagram/Facebook APIs and comply with their policies.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security, billing, or support.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues.

3. Legal Bases (where applicable)

We process data based on your consent, performance of a contract, legitimate interests (running and improving the service), and compliance with legal obligations.

4. Sharing

  • Service Providers: infrastructure, error monitoring, or customer support vendors under NDA and data-processing agreements.
  • Platform Partners: data flows to Meta platforms strictly via official APIs according to your permissions and actions.
  • Legal/Safety: if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or integrity.
  • We do not sell personal data.

5. Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 10).

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, access controls, least-privilege, audit logs). No method is 100% secure; residual risk remains.

7. Cookies

We may use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and session management. We do not run third-party ad trackers on our legal pages.

8. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the age defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries different from your residence. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

10. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion, and portability of your data.
  • Object or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise rights or request deletion, email legal@rimstudio.co or follow the steps at our Data Deletion page.

11. Instagram/Facebook Specifics

  • We only access scopes you approve and only to provide requested features.
  • We do not transfer Instagram/Facebook data to third parties except service providers under contract or as required by law.
  • We delete user data upon request or after account disconnection, within a reasonable period.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
